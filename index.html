have a go at home

what's it all about?

Lockdown has made it difficult to access creative spaces, take classes and try things out. We have a few nice pottery wheels to borrow. We do (contactless) wheel delivery and provide some basic tools so you can pot about at home.

We want these wheels being used and bringing joy, so there is a sliding scale of payment. Just let us know which option works for you.

what's it cost?

It's been a rough couple of years, and we want everyone to be able to enjoy some hands on fun.
We just ask you to consider others when choosing your rate.
Paying full price when you can makes it more sustainable to provide this experience to those who can't afford it.

  • standard - 100% (full price)
  • community - 50% (half price)
  • therapy - 0% (free)
Our aim is to put profits into creative ways to bust the lockdown blues.

make a booking

While we get a booking system sorted, send us an email at potabout@gmail.com.
Be sure to include:

  • preferred start and end dates
  • which rate (standard / community / therapy)
  • your approximate address (for delivery)
  • any thing else you'd like to add or ask

we'll get back to you with some other details and sort out a time for delivery.
